Chilli-mac

Ingredients

  • 2 cup elbow macroni
  • 2 pound ground beef
  • 2 small onion
  • 2 cup celery
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 4 cans tomato soup
  • 4 cans diced tomatoes
  • salt and pepper

  1. Cook the pasta for 8-9 minutes in boiling salt water
  2. Drain the pasta
  3. In sauce pan, add celery and green pepper with water
